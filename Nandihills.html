Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills is a picturesque hill station near Bangalore, Karnataka, India. It offers breathtaking views, stunning sunrises, and pleasant weather. The historical Nandi temple and Tipu's Drop are major attractions. The hill is perfect for trekking and paragliding, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  • Location:Chikkaballapur district of the state of Karnataka, India.
  • Best Time to Visit: October to February
  • Recommended Duration: Day Trip: Ideal for those who want to enjoy the sunrise, the main attractions, and outdoor activities in a single day. Overnight Stay: Suitable for a more leisurely visit, allowing you to explore Nandi Hills at a slower pace and spend time amidst the natural beauty and serenity of the hills.
